Rise & Shine Fruit & Nut Cereal

Organic, gluten-free and vegan! Go try it!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup whole sprouted spelt
  • 1 TBSP chia seed
  • 6 raw walnut halves chopped
  • 9 raw almonds chopped
  • 3 raw Brazil nuts chopped
  • 9 raw cashews chopped
  • 3/4 cup berries
  • 1 small banana chopped
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk (optional)

Steps

  • All ingredients added to a bowl,
    pour almond milk over and enjoy!

Cereal Chicken

This has always been our family favorite dish
when going to a restaurant.
It's tasty and kids love it.

Ingredients

  • 6 small chicken thigh (cut diced)
  • 1 TBSP chia seed
  • 6 raw walnut halves chopped
  • 9 raw almonds chopped
  • 3 raw Brazil nuts chopped
  • 9 raw cashews chopped
  • 3/4 cup berries
  • 1 small banana chopped
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened almond milk (optional)

Steps

  • Marinade the chicken for about 30 minutes,
    then deep fried.
  • Heat a pan, put on butter, fry the curry leaves
    and chilli.
    Then add the cereal in. Add some sugar for
    sweeter taste, and stir to avoid burn.
